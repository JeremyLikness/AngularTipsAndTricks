Performance

This example builds on the previous integration example by showing how to tweak and instrument performance. The "tweaks" involve using bind-once to ensure extraneous watches aren't created for the Fibonacci sequence. The "instrumentation" is performed by zone. In the console you can see generic performance information and the associated call stack. The incidents are fired based on the intervals for computation of the sequence followed by related animation kicked off as a result.
